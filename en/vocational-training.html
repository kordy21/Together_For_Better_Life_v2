Vocational Training

A project aimed at empowering individuals, especially youth and women, by providing them with practical vocational skills that enable them to enter the job market or start small businesses. The training covers various fields such as sewing, carpentry, beauty, handicrafts, and simple industries, and is conducted under the supervision of professionals to ensure high-quality and effective training. This project contributes to enhancing economic independence, reducing unemployment rates, and fostering a spirit of initiative and productivity within the community
