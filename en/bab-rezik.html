Training creates a golden opportunity for individuals seeking a way to learn a trade that can become a source of income. Vocational Training offers many advantages, including teaching trainees professional skills in a short period — skills that might take others a long time to acquire. Example: Plumbing and Carpentry Training For instance, in the "Lifeboats Initiative" (Plumbing), young people are trained for one month in the plumbing trade through both theoretical and practical sessions. At the end of the training, participants receive a toolkit containing plumbing equipment, in collaboration with the company EGYPTIAN GERMAN INDUSTRIAL CORPORATION (EGIC), helping open a livelihood opportunity for the trainees.
