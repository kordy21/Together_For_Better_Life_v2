Financial literacy seminar

Help individuals and families in villages better understand how to manage their financial resources through interactive sessions and practical tips for building a stable future.

Preparation and organization

On a bright morning, the association's team began preparing a simple hall in the heart of Al-Nour village in Beni Suef governorate. Chairs were arranged, introductory banners were hung, and paper materials to be distributed to attendees were prepared.

Reception and Registration of Attendees

As the time for the seminar drew near, the villagers began to arrive gradually. The entry process was organized, where each participant registered their name and received a simple booklet containing the basic concepts of financial literacy.

Start of the Awareness Session

The seminar opened with a welcoming speech, followed by an interactive session led by a specialized financial literacy trainer. The session covered concepts such as the difference between income and expenses, how to prepare a monthly budget, and the importance of saving even with a limited income.

Conclusion and Recommendations

The seminar concluded with a summary of the most important points. Written summaries of the content presented were distributed, along with recommendations to continue this type of awareness session.
