Exploration of Needy Areas

The Evaluation Department explores areas that appear to be in need of assistance. The Research Department conducts detailed field research on the cases of people living in these areas to collect all available data on the condition of their homes. Finally, researchers determine the eligible recipients and inspect the condition of their houses.

Registration of Cases in the Database

An employee at the "Together for a better life" institution registers the eligible areas and individuals in the database. Then, they provide services to the registered individuals through the charitable organization located in the village and registered with them. Alternatively, they work on developing basic services in the targeted village.

Providing assistance to those in need

After collecting data for the most deserving poor villages and neighborhoods that lack basic services, work begins in the village to provide its essential needs, such as roofing, water connections, electricity connections, and renovation of dilapidated homes, once a portion of donations from individuals or companies becomes available.
